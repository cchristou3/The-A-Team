Produce a test plan to address the following questions:
• Who is going to test your app?
• What specific aspects will they test?
• When will they do the testing?
• How will the results be recorded?
• What happens when a defect (bug) is discovered?
• How is the defect allocated to the right person?
• How will you trigger re-testing of the code?
